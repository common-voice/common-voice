A Thanaiste, A Airi, a Ard vhaara, agus a dhaoune uaishe.

Is Mise Reece Smyth, Ambasador gniomhach vMeirica faoi lathair.
Is onuir mór domsa labhairt libh inniu.
Ta suil agam go mbaineann sibh sult as an oiche.
